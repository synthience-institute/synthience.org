Practitioner Guides

Practitioner Guides are operational documents for professionals who work with AI systems. They are not academic papers. Each guide addresses a specific, real problem: what goes wrong, why it happens structurally, what the risk surface looks like, and what to do about it. They are the applied layer of the Institute's research, written for the people who actually have to make AI work reliably inside organizations.

The gateway guide. If you are using conversational AI for anything that matters and you want it to actually work consistently, this is where to start. Covers the core problem of context degradation, why it happens, and what reliable working practice looks like.
